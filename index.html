About

Hi, I'm Gaurav Bhalla and this is my personal website. Here, you can find a few of my projects and my blog. I'm currently working on breaking into the web development industry, and I have a keen interest in politics and economics alongside conputer science. In order to further pursue these interests, I read widely, making sure to follow both local and global news.

In an attempt to further pursue these interests, I also take part in Model UN, I began only a few months ago and have taken part in two weekend conferences, being a commended delegate in the first and highly commended in the second. I'm aiming to win outstanding delegate at my next conference.

As for web development, I mainly do websites, front end with pure code and both back and frontend with a CMS. I first got involved in python, the first language I picked up that I truly tried to learn and become proficient in. That later developed to Java, then JavaScript, from which point I moved onto web development. I'm looking into some backend languages, but I would rather not use python. I also want to learn app development, having made a few apps before that I successfully ran at least on my phone.

I'm also interested in maths, although I prefer algebra over geometry. I like maths because of how each step follows on from the previous with logic, you can work things out from reasoning alone.

- Gaurav Bhalla
